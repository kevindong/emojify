Instructions

Note that you need an emoji in your Slack called "empty" (which should be a plain white image) in order for this tool to work correctly. Click here to download such an image. You'll need to upload it to your Slack in order to use this tool.

Inputs
Alphanumeric (upper or lower case A to Z and any number 0-9) inputs only. Spaces are allowed.
